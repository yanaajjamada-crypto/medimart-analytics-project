What the data actually says.

🏆

Supplements dominate revenue — ₹6,150, nearly 3× Diabetes and 36× Pain Relief. A supplements-led growth story, not a prescription-led one.

👑

One customer, outsized impact — Yana Ajjamada alone drives ~49% of completed revenue, and is the only "Loyal" customer with 3+ orders.

📉

20% cancellation rate — 2 of 10 orders cancelled, both from customers who never returned. Worth investigating at real scale.

🌆

No cross-city overlap — every city's top spender is a different person, pointing to distributed demand, not one dominant hub.
